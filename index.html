1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Data You Provide

  • Task and Note Content: The tasks, notes, reminders, and other content you create within the App

  • Feedback and Support: Information you provide when contacting us for support or feedback

1.3 Optional Permissions

  • Notifications: To send reminders and task notifications
  • Storage: To save and backup your tasks locally
  • Calendar: To sync tasks with your device calendar (if enabled)
  • Camera: To attach photos to tasks (if you choose to use this feature)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To provide task management, reminders, and organizational features
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve app performance and features
  • Technical Support: To troubleshoot issues and provide customer support
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide updates about the App

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Your tasks and personal data are primarily stored locally on your device. We implement encryption and secure storage practices to protect your information.

3.2 Cloud Backup (Optional)

If you enable cloud backup features, your data may be stored securely in encrypted cloud storage services. You can disable this feature at any time in the app settings.

3.3 Security Measures

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure development practices and code reviews
